# Core Behavior Rules

- Optimize for correctness and memory safety over speed.
- Prefer simple implementations over complex abstractions.
- Do not overengineer.
- Do not introduce unnecessary features.
- Make the smallest change that solves the task.
- Do not refactor unrelated code.
- Keep solutions runnable in Google Colab.

- Always consider time, direction, and amount in transaction logic.
- Do not simplify the problem into a static graph without justification.
